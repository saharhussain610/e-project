Key Publications by Albert Einstein

Zur Elektrodynamik bewegter Körper

This 1905 paper by Einstein describes the special theory of relativity and marks the beginning of modern physics.

Die Grundlage der allgemeinen Relativitätstheorie

In this 1915 paper, Einstein presents the general theory of relativity, revolutionizing our understanding of gravity.

Die Quantentheorie des Lichts

This 1905 paper by Einstein explains the concept of the photon, which played a key role in the development of quantum theory.
